The Process of Balancing Chemical Equations

Balancing chemical equations is the process of adjusting the coefficients in a chemical equation to ensure that the number of atoms of each element is the same on both sides of the equation.

Example:

C + O₂ → CO₂

This equation needs balancing!

Key Steps:
  • 1. Count atoms on each side
  • 2. Add coefficients to balance
  • 3. Check your work
Why Important:

Balanced equations follow the law of conservation of mass.
